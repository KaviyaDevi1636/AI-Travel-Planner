

## AI Travel Planner - College Project

A modern, minimal travel planning app that generates personalized itineraries with budget estimates and interactive maps.

---

### 🏠 Landing Page
- Hero section with engaging headline and search interface
- Quick destination input with travel dates and preferences
- Call-to-action to start planning
- Clean, modern design with travel-themed imagery

---

### 🤖 Trip Planner Page
**Input Form:**
- Destination search
- Travel dates (start & end)
- Number of travelers
- Trip type (Adventure, Relaxation, Cultural, etc.)
- Budget range selector

**Generated Itinerary:**
- Day-by-day breakdown of activities
- Recommended places to visit
- Suggested restaurants and accommodations
- Time estimates for each activity

---

### 💰 Budget Estimation
- Breakdown by category (accommodation, food, activities, transport)
- Visual progress bars or charts
- Total estimated cost display
- Currency display

---

### 🗺️ Interactive Map
- Google Maps integration showing:
  - All destinations on the itinerary
  - Markers for attractions, hotels, restaurants
  - Route visualization between locations

---

### 💾 Saved Trips
- View all previously saved trip plans
- Delete unwanted trips
- Reload and view past itineraries
- Data persisted in browser's local storage
